For Solo Mode:

Press W to move forwards. S to move backwards.

A to turn counter clockwise. D to turn clockwise.

SpaceBar to shoot.

For Multi-Player Mode:

The Left Player uses WASD to move.

Press W to move forwards. S to move backwards.

A to turn counter clockwise. D to turn clockwise.

SpaceBar to shoot.

The Right Player uses the arrow keys:

Up for forwards, Down for backwards,

Left for counter clockwise, Right for clockwise and Enter to shoot.
